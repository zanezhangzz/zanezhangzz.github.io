"Free YouTube Video Summary" Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-08-28

Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use that information, and the steps we take to ensure your personal information is handled appropriately.

Personal Data

We do not collect any of your personal data.

Non-Personal Data

We use chrome.storage.local or window.localStorage to save your configuration settings for this extension, but this data is not synced to any remote server.

We collect the following data generated from your use of this extension through our network:

  • The YouTube video URL you want to summarize
  • The AI website you choose to navigate to
  • The AI model you select from the AI website

This data is used to navigate to third-party websites to perform video summaries.

Data Sharing

We respect your privacy. We do not share your personal or non-personal data with any third parties, except as required by law.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of the page.
