Install on Chrome Store

"Free YouTube Video Summary" is a simple and lightweight Chrome extension.

Now, Gemini can directly summarize video content, even if the video doesn't have subtitles.

This plugin allows you to send the current video's URL to Gemini with a single click for automatic submission.

You can also customize your prompts and choose your preferred model.

In my tests, I found that besides Gemini, Grok can handle some videos, but it isn't very stable. ChatGPT performs even more poorly.

I've decided to keep the options for Grok and ChatGPT, as AI is advancing rapidly. They will likely support this functionality soon.
